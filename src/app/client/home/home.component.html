Get The Real Story

Most managers believe employees leave because of 'pull' factors such as better pay, rather than 'push' factors that occur long before employees look for greener pastures. Workforce Retention specialises in uncovering what happened when an employee first makes that decision to leave.

Insightful Exit Reporting

Find the top reasons for leaving quickly and efficiently via our simple chart-based reporting platform. Our exit interviews are structured in such a way that reasons for leaving are pushed to the top and ranked in order of what has the greatest propensity for employees to leave.
