Flexible
Exit Interview
Program Options
Workforce Retention offers flexible exit interview programs with a comprehensive, easy-to-use platform. Professional phone exit interviews can be offered as an 'add on' to the platform offering, or as a stand-alone service depending on business needs and budget. Take a look at some of the ways we can structure the right program for you:
1

easy-to-use
platform


The platform offers the ability to run phone, online or internal face to face interviews. It can be administered internally or used only to view individual and full exit reports. Features:

  • Set up or upload exiting employee profiles and control the mode of exit interview i.e. phone, online or face to face .
  • Set-up and update anytime the default email script for distribution to exiting employees.
  • Passwords can be reset for online exit interviews.
  • Auto-reminder email sent to those set up to complete an online interview.
  • Save and send individual exit reports via PDF to managers by email.
  • Online charting and reports that can be filtered by division, gender, tenure
  • Access to full data extracts for own analysis at any time.
2

expert phone
exit interviews


We specialise in making the experience as professional and engaging as it can be, leaving employees with a sense of fulfilment while

  • Our exit Interviews are designed to capture insightful information across all aspects of employment.
  • We avoid recording subjective information. Rather than record the interview conversation verbatim, we summarise responses in a helpful narrative.
  • We listen, empathise and make the employee feel valued and heard.
  • Where exiting employees make a judgement about an aspect of their time, we seek specific examples.
  • We encourage employees to tell us what could have happened differently to make their experience more positive.
  • At completion, the interview is reviewed and finalised. The report can be emailed to HR, Managers or simply access it from the platform and download to PDF,
3

online chart
reporting


The unique exit interview structure is designed to rank top reasons for leaving categories, and showcase the specific areas of concern in dynamic charts. Eg: if it’s Leadership - is it communication or values? If it’s pay, is it about fairness or benefits?

  • Online charts are indexed every time an interview is completed.
  • Filter time periods, gender, tenure, organizational level and position level.
  • Provide access to chart reports to business or HR leaders, while maintaining anonymity for other divisions.
  • Choose confidential or non-confidential. If confidential, the charts will not display until enough responses assure anonymity.
  • Download reports to PDF
  • Understand ‘What’s Working Well’ and ‘What’s Not Working Well’ with charts illuminating the top 10 and bottom 10 ranked statements in the interview.
  • Access to full data extracts for own analysis at any time.
4

Comprehensive
reporting


Full exit reports are the result of expert analysis. These reports are usually produced quarterly and are highly valuable because the mining, refining and summarising of the data is all taken care of. Features include:

  • Full analysis on all quantitative and qualitative aspects of the interview. These reports are usually produced quarterly.
  • Participation summary and trend analysis on current period versus last period.
  • Top reasons for leaving ranked and explored through deep analysis of employee commentary.
  • Drill down analysis on ‘hot spots’ uncovered through analysis.
  • Analysis on other groups talent ie: generational/ performance/ job level, salary banding/ women & diversity available.
  • Reports are uploaded as PDF to the platform or emailed direct to nominated staff.
