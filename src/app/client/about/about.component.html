Angeline’s Story

One sunny afternoon in 2002, I was working for a large corporate company in Sydney. I had to pull together workforce metrics to contribute to a Board Report. One of the items to include in the report was the ‘top reasons people leave’ and knowing an exit interview process was currently in place, I confidently requested the latest exit interview report.

I was really surprised to find the information so lacking in objective, helpful information about why employees leave. Knowing the significant cost of voluntary turnover to business bottom line I thought, what a missed opportunity!

That afternoon I had a ‘light bulb’ moment. If an effective exit interview program was in place where information was harnessed sensitively, captured centrally, analysed and reported objectively, what powerful insight would ensue for HR and business leaders.

That was the beginning of an amazing journey. Starting as a research project into employee behaviour when on maternity leave, I used my HR data background to pilot a program with AMP using a Microsoft Access database. This soon became an online program which has been tweaked and perfected over many years.



Angeline Wilkes

About Workforce Retention

We are now a virtual, boutique business built around supporting organisations with their unique exit interview programs. The programs are built around three distinct principles.

First, ensuring the ‘so what’ factors form part of the design of the interview process, so results point to meaningful action for businesses. This principle permeates the entire design of the exit interview process from the questions we ask, through to producing invaluable reports.

Second, having the ability to quickly view the top reasons for leaving by group or division, while at the same time being able to offer expert analysis on the qualitative aspects of the interviews. Those that can’t be charted but need a human to analyse themes in commentary to qualify what the charts are showing.

Finally, making the experience as professional and engaging as it can be where employees have a safe space to contribute their experience. This gives them a sense of purpose and closure. It is so important at such a significant juncture in their lives.

Our programs have achieved over 95% participation.

I’m so excited that we’ve now launched a new platform on the latest technology stack. If you have a license to the platform you can opt in to our Application Programming Interface (API) for additional value. This means if you have other data visualisation software, you can pull through the top reasons for leaving alongside other business performance metrics.
