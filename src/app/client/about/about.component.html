About Workforce Retention

We are a boutique business that has been delivering effective, flexible exit interview programs since 2008. Here are 3 key features at the heart of what we do:

  1. Provide meaningful, insightful results that are actionable for business. Starting from the collaborative design of the exit interview, integrating neuroscience techniques, through to scheduling, interviewing and finally producing valuable reports.

  2. Flexible drill through reporting plus expert analysis. Businesses can quickly view the top reasons for leaving by group or division, while at the same get a helpful narrative of the charts with our comprehensive exit reporting. Having an expert analyse themes in the commentary to qualify what the charts are showing often provides a crucial missing link.

  3. We offer a professional, connected and engaging experience for exiting employees. A safe space so employees can contribute their experience that provides a sense of purpose and closure. This is vitally important at such a significant juncture in their lives.

Some years ago, I had to pull together workforce metrics to contribute to a Board Report. One of the items to include was the 'top reasons people leave'. I was astonished to find the organisation did not have objective, helpful information about why employees leave.

Knowing the high cost of voluntary turnover to business bottom line I thought, what a missed opportunity!

Through research I learned the limitations of many internally run exit interview programs including; exit interviews being run sporadically, low trust, exit interview information not collected centrally, results not analysed objectively, results being filed and forgotten.

The most common limitation was that psychological safety is not considered a priority and so, many missed opportunities to gather the real causes of employee turnover.

In the years that followed an exit interview program was developed, tested and perfected before being built onto one of the latest technology cloud platforms.

Using some of the latest neuroscience research, we now incorporate techniques in the exit interviews to help employees feel safe and heard. Once we have that it's about guiding the exiting employee out of the detail, problem and drama and into the planning, solution and visioning part of the brain where we can understand and record insightful suggestions and improvements for the organisation.

The insight from the data is compelling. It helps businesses understand the drivers of employee turnover and leaves employees feeling they've been heard and valued. It also leaves the employer brand in a more positive shape.

The flexible platform allows organisations to either run internal face to face interviews, administer an online exit interview or outsource the entire service to us where we have a talented team of women who expertly administer and conduct phone exit interviews.

Our programs have achieved over 95% participation.
